bear Adventures

bear Adventures is an original comic strip by BIA at Berkeley featuring four bears. The four bears have very different personalities and represent every student at UC Berkeley. The bears will encounter various difficulties and surprises in school with us. Their stories don't just take place on campus, but also in their hometown, Bear Village, where many exciting stories take place.
